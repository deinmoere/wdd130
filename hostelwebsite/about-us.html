About Us

Your comfort our priority

Enyonam and Ami started DreAmi Hostels to provide tourists affordable accommodation giving tourists a hassle-free experience as they tour the attraction sites in Accra. That's why our websites can be viewed in 19 languages. We want to be the first choice of Solo travellers (Nomads, explorers, wanderers, hippies), Backpacking enthusiasts, Students, Group of young people (age 18 and above) attending any events or festivals, or Foreigners.

At DreAmi Hostels

It's nice to feel at home, but it's also nice to feel warmth and well being during your holiday. DreAmi Hostels has the beauty of an unique apartments, with, at the same time, both the comfort of a house and a hotel. The rooms have fresh sheets, the beds are of a very good quality and the towels are always soft and scented. We guarantee the maintenance and cleaning of the rooms. We have tried to furnish and put into the rooms everything we thought could be useful to make you feel good, but if you are missing something, try asking us! You will always find us ready to please all your needs. We respect the needs of our clients because this is not just a place to visit, this is your home away from home.
