Overview

Purpose

Have you ever visited the websites of hotels or hostels near tourist attraction cites? How easy was it to navigate through the site? Well, as a web developer, creating online presence for businesses that house or accommodate travellers is what I do. Picture this, a website that displays the services the hostel has to offer alongside a display of the various room categories available. Amazing right? Wouldn't it be more amazing if the site allowed you to book for a room space right from the comfort of your home? My proposed website will do all these and more, giving you a hassle-free experience as you tour the attraction cites of Accra, Ghana.

Audience

The target customers are people looking for affordable accommodation as they tour the attraction sites in Accra. Our customers may be Solo travellers (Nomads, explorers, wanderers, hippies), Backpacking enthusiasts, Students, Group of young people (age 18 and above) attending any events or fests or Foreigners. Here at DreAmi Hostels, we provide customers with a homy feel environment and a tranquil environment to relax after they tour Accra city. We have wonderful room packages for families, individuals, and groups.
