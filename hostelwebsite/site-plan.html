Overview

Purpose

Have you ever visited the websites of hotels or hostels near tourist attraction cites? How easy was it to navigate through the site? Well, as a web developer, creating online presence for businesses that house or accommodate travellers is what I do. Picture this, a website that displays the services the hostel has to offer alongside a display of the various room categories available. Amazing right? Wouldn't it be more amazing if the site allowed you to book for a room space right from the comfort of your home? My proposed website will do all these and more, giving you a hassle-free experience as you tour the attraction sites of Accra, Ghana.

Home page

Tour the beautiful attraction sites of Accra with us
Travelling on a budget and looking for affordable accommodation as you tour the attraction sites in Accra? Wondering if staying at a hostel is worth the experience? Welcome to DreAmi Hostels, a home away from home. We provide a tranquil homey feel environment to relax after you tour the beautiful city of Accra.

Services and Amenities

Safety: Here at DreAmi Hostels, alongside comfort, the safety of our customers is our utmost priority. We have security personnels as well as a front desk operating 24/7, ensuring that our customers feel safe and secure at all times.
Lockers: We know fully well customers come with valuables and most times need a place to store them. Here at DreAmi Hostels, personal lockers are provided for our cherished customers to store their valuables during their stay.
Room and board: We have wonderful room packages for families, individuals, and groups. Rooms can be booked right from the comfort of your home or by calling our booking department to make reservations.
Common Areas: Our hostel is equipped with secure access to living areas, common lounge areas, Kitchenette with microwaves, free WiFi and 24/7 laundry facilities.
Why Accra
Accra is a vibrant city along the coastline of Ghana, with amazing tourist attractions. It is an ideal tourist destination with a fantastic climate and a conducive environment for relaxation. There are several reasons why you should make Accra your next holiday destination. Accra has the following amazing activities: For lovers of art, Accra has wonderful galleries of all kinds containing such a diverse array of art.
Sports lovers choose Accra for their holidays because the city offers a wide range of sports. The major games offered in Accra include Polo, horseback riding, rugby, surfing, and street hockey. The city also has other activities that tourists can participate in to ensure that they remain physically fit.
The main traditional dishes include banku, veggie plantain and beans, fufu, and different types of soups. Jollof is among the best traditional dishes prepared in Ghana. Most tourists who like traditional foods usually prefer to travel to Accra to taste the best traditional meals. The dishes can be found in local roadside stalls, open-air Bush Canteen, and restaurants.
Accra city offers numerous venues where people can enjoy live music performances. It is exciting to listen to Afro-based music in unique venues. Rooms

Attending an event or festival? Family vacation? With the crew? Solo backpacker? No worries, you will find the perfect accommodation for you at DreAmi Hostels. Our rooms are equipped with high speed Internet access, television with cable, closet with storage rack. Whether staying in a private room or a dormitory, you will be sure to have sweet dreams.
Dormitories: The classic youth hostel dorm - completely redesigned for a safe and secure stay. The layout may vary, but it'll always be stylish and friendly with access to a dedicated bathroom. Each bed is ultra-comfortable, with a USB port, bedside lamp and secure locker within easy reach. Private rooms: Like a hotel, with the fun atmosphere of a youth hostel. Our rooms are ideal for travellers looking for a bit of peace and quiet and are all equipped with ultra-comfortable beds and access to a private bathroom.
